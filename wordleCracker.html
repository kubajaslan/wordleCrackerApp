Wordle Cracker

How To Play

Write the word you chose in the Wordle game and select the boxes which were lit in yellow or green by clicking on them.

GREEN = letter is in the word and is in the correct spot.

YELLOW = letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.

WHITE = letter is not in the word in any spot.

When you answer is ready, click Evaluate.

You can find the original game here: nytimes.com/games/wordle
